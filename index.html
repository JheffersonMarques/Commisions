Terms of Service

  • Delivery and Pricing : Pricing and estimated time until devilery can change depening on mod complexity;
  • Payment : A commission will be worked upon after a payment of at least 50% the agreed upon price, with the other 50% being due within a week of the final delivery;
  • Progress : Progress will be shared with the commissioner, so it could be iterated upon, and until marked as done, any feature can be changed;
  • Changes on approved features : Features marked as done, will be charged separetely to be changed;
  • Additional Features : Any additional features not outlined in the initial agreement will be charged separetely, with cost being defined in the same rate as before;
  • Usage : Mods can be used in anyway (be it personal or comercial use) with credit attributed;
  • Publishing : Mods commisioned can be uploaded to mod agregation sites like CurseForge and Modrinth with a revenue split of 25% to 75% (25% for me, 75% for the commissioner) after 3 months at minimum unless otherwise specified.
  • Refunds : Refunds can be made in any step of the process, meaning the commission work will not be delivered, and the non worked hours will be refunded (for example. a 40 hours project that has been worked on for 8 hours, will recieve the remaining 32 hours as a refund), once the commissioner approved a delivery, they can no longer ask for refunds;
  • Source Code : Any mods made via commissions will be made open source after 3-6 months under the MIT License;
